-> 1) What is Salesforce?

Salesforce is a cloud platform that helps companies manage their customer data and business operations.

It is mainly used for:

- Managing customers
- Tracking sales
- Customer support
- Marketing
- Business automation

Since it is cloud-based, users can access it from anywhere.

Example:
A company can store customer details, follow-up information, and service requests in one platform.


-> 2) What is CRM?

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.

It helps businesses manage communication with customers.

CRM stores information like:

- Customer name
- Contact details
- Purchase history
- Complaints
- Sales information

Without CRM:
Companies may store everything in Excel sheets or emails.

With CRM:
Everything is organized in one place.


-> 3) Why companies use Salesforce?

Companies use Salesforce because it helps them work more efficiently.

Benefits:

- Stores customer data
- Improves customer relationships
- Automates repetitive tasks
- Tracks sales progress
- Generates reports
- Saves time

Salesforce is popular because it is:

- Cloud-based
- Secure
- Flexible
- Easy to customize


-> 4) Important Salesforce Terms

-> Account

Account represents a company or organization.

Examples:

- Infosys
- Wipro
- Amazon

It stores company-related information.


-> Contact

Contact represents a person related to an account.

Examples:

- Customer
- Employee
- Manager

It stores details like:

- Name
- Email
- Phone number


-> Lead

Lead is a person who shows interest in a product/service.

Example:
Someone filling out a website inquiry form.


-> Opportunity

Opportunity represents a possible business deal.

It helps track:

- Deal amount
- Stage
- Probability

Example:
A company planning to buy software services.

6) Salesforce Developer Role

A Salesforce Developer builds custom solutions on Salesforce.

They work on:

Apex coding
Lightning components
APIs
Custom objects
Automation

They help companies customize Salesforce based on their requirements.
